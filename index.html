Who We Are

Founded in 2022, L&F Logistics was built from a passion for trucking and a vision to create something bigger than just moving freight.

Founder Ronnie Lee began his journey in 2019 as a driver and quickly grew into ownership, founding Reliable Hauling LLC, a hotshot trucking company that now operates a fleet of five trucks. That hands-on experience behind the wheel laid the foundation for how L&F Logistics operates today.

With the support of his wife Amanda and co-owner Seth, Ronnie expanded into freight brokerage—launching L&F Logistics, a family-owned, relationship-driven business that connects shippers with trusted, dependable carriers.

At L&F Logistics, we move everything from hotshot loads to semis, including step decks, flatbeds, and dry vans. Whether your freight moves on our own fleet through Reliable Hauling or via our carefully vetted carrier partners, every load is handled with care, honesty, and efficiency.

Based in Indianapolis, we serve all 48 states with a focus on reliability and long-term partnerships.
